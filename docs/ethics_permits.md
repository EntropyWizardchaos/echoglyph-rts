# Ethics & Permit Checklist (US‑centric; adapt to locale)

- **Permits:** Marine Mammal Protection Act (MMPA), Endangered Species Act (ESA) as applicable; NOAA/NMFS authorization; IACUC protocol.
- **Partnerships:** accredited marine labs/stranding networks; vet oversight.
- **Noninvasive only:** suction‑cup tags; no implants; no tail hardware.
- **Playback controls (if any):** SPL limits by species, duty cycle, maximum daily exposure; pre‑registered stop criteria.
- **Data governance:** minimize PII of researchers/locations where needed; share aggregate results responsibly; adhere to local and indigenous guidelines for marine life interactions.
