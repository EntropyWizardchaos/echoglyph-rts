# Test Plan — Bench → Flume → Tank → Field

## Phase 0 — Bench (2–4 weeks)
- **Audio chain:** calibrate hydrophone noise floor; click detection ROC.
- **R–T–S model:** train on public sperm‑whale codas; round‑trip synthesis test ≥80% within‑set ID.
- **Flex harvest:** shaker table on couplers; verify 10–100 µW average into supercap at expected bend rates.
- **Release:** burn‑wire timing; VHF beacon range (dry).

## Phase 1 — Flume
- Dummy hydrodynamic shell + cups; measure drag @ 0.5–1.5 m/s; suction retention; clean release.
- Tune segment stiffness and cup layout; confirm height ≤ 30 mm.

## Phase 2 — Tank (partner facility)
- Suction acceptance on surrogate surfaces; slip under turns/roll.
- Audio self‑noise in bubbly/turbulent conditions.
- Optional managed odontocete exposure for short passive logging; *no playback yet*.

## Phase 3 — Field (permitted)
- Short (12–48 h) deployments with pop‑off + recovery.
- Log codas, depth, IMU; confirm R–T–S rendering and latent clustering offline.
- No playback unless explicitly approved by IACUC/permit.

## Metrics & stop criteria
- Drag < 10 N @ 1.5 m/s (target). Suction loss rate < 1 per hour.
- Audio SNR > 15 dB on median clicks at 10 m.
- Release success ≥ 99%. Clean recovery ≥ 95%.
- Animal behavior monitoring: if agitation rises, abort and revise.
